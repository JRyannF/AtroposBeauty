Aftercare Instructions

Brow Lamination

Duration: Results typically last 4–6 weeks.

  • 24 Hour Rule: Do not wet your eyebrows for 24 hours.
  • Steam & Water Caution: Avoid hot showers, saunas, steam rooms, and pools.
  • Makeup Warning: No eye makeup for 12 hours. Use oil-free remover if needed.
  • Hands Off: Avoid touching or rubbing your brows.
  • Sun & Skincare: Limit sun, avoid chlorine and active skincare on brows.

Microblading

  • Keep Brows Dry: No water for 10 days.
  • Dry Healing Only: Avoid ointments like Aquaphor or Neosporin.
  • Face Product Precautions: Keep skin products away from brows.
  • Skip the Sweat: Avoid exercise and sweat for 10 days.
  • No Picking or Scratching: Let scabs fall off naturally.
  • Sun Protection: Protect from direct sun to prevent fading.
  • Prevent Infection: Treat as an open wound to avoid contamination.
